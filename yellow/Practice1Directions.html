Practice Question 1: Birthday Cake

Practice Scenario 1

You are trying to choose a cake that you want to buy for your friend’s birthday. You are considering the following traits:

Sweetness:

This is important because you know your friend’s taste and you want to make sure the cake has the perfect amount of sweetness

Design:

This is important because you want to make sure the cake is pleasing to look at

Size:

This is important because you want to buy the perfect size of the cake that will feed everyone but won’t leave too much leftover.

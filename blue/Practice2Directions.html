You are trying to choose a restaurant to go to with a group of friends. You are considering the following attributes:

Atmosphere:

This is important because you want to make sure you want to feel cozy and at home while dining out with your friends.

Food Quality:

This is important because you want to make sure everyone there will enjoy the food.

Distance:

This is important because you want to make sure everyone finds parking spot without any hassle.

**Only consider information that you think is important, rather than focusing on all information, for your efficient decision-making.**

Note that Atmosphere and Food Quality are the attributes that are considered to be most important by many others including experts.

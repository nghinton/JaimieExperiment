Start of Experiment

In this questionnaire, you will be asked to make choices between several alternatives.

The attributes of these products will be hidden behind boxes. You can look at the attributes by clicking the mouse pointer on the box. The box will open and you can see the information, until you click another box.

The following practice task is designed to help you become familiar with clicking the mouse on the boxes to view specific information. Look at the information in the boxes, and when you are ready, please click the button that says “make decision”, to indicate your decision.

First we will go over the instructions in more detail, then we will start the practice trials.
